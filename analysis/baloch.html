1. Language, Culture & National Responsibility

Theme Description

These questions focus on the role of language in national culture and responsibility. They ask candidates to comment on the importance of protecting and promoting the language, its cultural significance, and the duties of society in this regard.

(20 marks)

4. Technical & Aesthetic Aspects of Balochi Language & Literature

Theme Description

These items focus on discussing both technical (linguistic/structural) and aesthetic aspects of the Balochi language and its literature. This includes analysis of language style, poetic techniques, and the technical presentation of literary works.

  • 2019 – PART-II, Question 5:

"جہل و انتگیں بلوچی زبان و شاعراں چہ دو از می و تیکنیکی پہنا تانی سر انبشه به کن۔"
(20 marks)
(Sub-items provide answer options such as: (i) جو انسال بگٹی, (ii) غلام حسین شوباز, (iii) عطا شاد)

Preparation Insights

  • Translation Skills:
    Practice translating both technical and literary texts between English and Balochi. Emphasize idiomatic expressions and cultural nuance.
  • Critical Essay Writing:
    Develop structured outlines for essays on literary criticism and cultural narratives. Compare classical texts with modern trends and support your arguments with examples.
  • Debate & Identity:
    Read widely on Balochi language history and cultural identity debates. Be prepared to articulate your position regarding the origins and social role of Balochi language and literature.
  • Proverbs:
    Build a glossary of common Balochi proverbs and practice their translation to and from English.
